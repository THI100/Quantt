# Quantt
A trading bot based on a hybrid approach of AI and tradiotinal analysis methods. Areas of risk, analisys and others are relationated to AI, but do not purely rely on it.

- AI Models:
    - TTM granite R2.0: A timeseries model for forecasting candles and the market movement, based on diverse inputs. Fine tuned to crypto markets and their data.
    - Gemini 3.0 flash/ChatGPT 5.0 mini: Used for news analisys and market sentiment.
    - Custom Risk model: Variable risk, leading to a higher higher safety and profit.
